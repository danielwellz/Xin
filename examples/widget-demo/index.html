Xin Embed Widget

Copy/paste the snippet below into your site (SSR-safe, bilingual):

<script defer src="https://cdn.example.com/embed.js"
  data-tenant="TENANT_UUID"
  data-api="https://api.xinbot.ir"
  data-gateway="wss://gateway.xinbot.ir"
  data-locale="fa"></script>

For React apps import @xin-platform/widget-sdk/react and wrap your tree with XinWidgetProvider.
